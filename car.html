    VOLKSWAGEN


Passat

The Volkswagen Passat is a good midsize car that provides a cushioned ride, capable engine, roomy cabin, and large trunk. However, it's in a highly competitive segment full of great vehicles.

Tiguan

Volkswagen Tiguan places in the middle of our compact SUV rankings. The Tiguan has a comfortable, well-equipped cabin and a good predicted reliability rating, but its underwhelming engine and below-average fuel economy hold it back.

    TOYOTA


corolla sedan

With the base engine, the 2020 Corolla gets an EPA-estimated 30 mpg in the city and 38 mpg on the highway. Those fuel economy figures are good for a compact car. A CVT-equipped Corolla with the 2.0-liter engine is even more efficient, with excellent estimates of 31 mpg in the city and 40 mpg on the highway.

Prius

The Toyota Prius is a good choice among hybrid and electric cars. It has a capable hybrid system, a smooth ride, and unbelievably good fuel economy ratings. Inside, the Prius has two rows of spacious seats, user-friendly infotainment features, and more cargo room than most competitors.

Sienna

Toyota Sienna. As minivans go, the Sienna is a sensible choice, but it isn't very engaging to drive. Although the Sienna rides comfortably and is quiet inside, its handling is lackluster. It has a lively 3.5-liter V6, but the eight-speed automatic doesn't shift very smoothly.
